I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at Bocconi University. My primary research interests lie in Decision Theory, Stochastic Choice, Microeconomic Theory, and Behavioural Economics
Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a pre-doctoral researcher at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, contributing to a project on renewable energy and electricity markets from an industrial organization perspective.
My methodological foundations were shaped during my M.A. in Economics at Collegio Carlo Alberto in Turin, where I received rigorous training in economic theory and econometrics, a background that proved essential throughout my pre-doctoral experience.
